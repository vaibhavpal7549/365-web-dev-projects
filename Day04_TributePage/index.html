Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

Missile Man of India

Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and politician who served as the 11th president of India from 2002 to 2007. He played a crucial role in India's Pokhran-II nuclear tests and was known for his inspirational speeches, humility, and vision for youth empowerment.

  • Born: 15 October 1931
  • Died: 27 July 2015
  • Occupation: Scientist, President of India
  • Known For: Missile Development, Vision 2020
